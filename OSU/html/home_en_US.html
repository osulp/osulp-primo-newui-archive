Introducing the New 1Search

1Search provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

1Search also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create favorites lists
  • Export citations to Mendeley and Endnote Basic
  • Access full-text articles
